Hardware Components

    Each GaussSense board consists of 1 PCB board, 1 Microcontroller, 34 1-to-16 Multiplexers, and 16*32=512 Analog Hall Sensors.
  • PCB schemetic layout: (Filetype: Gerber, 4-layers PCB) We freely license the design of PCB layout for Individual and Educational uses with non-commercial purposes. Please fill the above form, and mail us to get the schematics.
  • Microcontroller: Teensy2 (Recommended), Arduino compatibles.
  • Multiplexer: NXP Semiconductors 74HCT4067 (SOT355-1, SSOP24 or TSSOP24 Package).
  • Analog Hall Sensor: Winson Analog Hall Sensor: WSH136/WSH137/WSH138 (SOT-23 Package).
