  • Rong-Hao received his Ph.D. in Computer Science (2014) and M.S. in Electrical Engineering (2010) from National Taiwan University. He is an Assistant Professor in Industrial Design Department and Electrical Engineering Department, Eindhoven University of Technology. He is also the co-founder of GaussToys Inc. Inc. He is interested in technical HCI research, focusing on sensing systems and rapid prototyping tools for applications in ubiquitous computing and human-computer interaction.
